An App Development Engineering Firm

Sevendyne is an app development engineering firm headquartered in Kerala, India. Since 2016, our team designs, builds, and ships production software — mobile apps, web platforms, embedded systems, and enterprise tools — for clients across the UK, Germany, Malaysia, Singapore, the US, and India.

This repository hosts our open-source Enterprise HRMS, built over one year by a small engineering team of three in our local office. It is one of several products we develop in-house while delivering custom engineering work for global clients.

Visit sevendyne.com for more details

  • Embedded & IoT software
  • Cloud, DevOps & integration services
  • QA, testing & delivery engineering

Our Story

Sevendyne began in 2016 as a focused engineering practice — assembling dedicated development teams and shipping real products, not slide decks. Today we combine client delivery with open-source contributions like this HRMS, inviting collaborators to fork, run, and improve our work. For company background, services, client work, and contact information, refer to our main website.
